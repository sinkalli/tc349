About La Bella Italiano

La Bella Italiano opened in 2012 and has long been the leader in contemporary Italian cuisine in everywhere, featuring regional Italian food and wine rooted primarily in the northern provincial tradition. Each month, Chef John Yourname creates several feature dishes uniquely specific to an Italian region. La Bella Italiano's physical structure enjoys a distinguished local history as the former Orpheum Theater, which screened the most popular movies of the day in the 1930s. This dramatic history is preserved today in the restaurant's elegant decor and ambience. Seated above La Bella Italiano's bar, a grand sculpture of Orpheus himself, the great poet and musician, overlooks the dining room guests, while a beautifully painted Renaissance-style mural depicting an ancient bacchanal reinforces the indulgent and purely pleasurable atmosphere.
