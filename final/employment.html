Employment

At La Bella Italiano,we are always looking for great people who want to be a part of our team. We strive to be the very best and to exceed our guests expectations so we look for the very best employees with a real commitment to the highest standards.

How to Apply:

  • In person: Daily between 2-4pm at the the restaurant. Please call in advance.
  • Fax:Fax a resume or an application to (517) 555-1111. Be sure to indicate what position(s) you're interested in.
  • Email:Email a resume: to employment@labellaitaliano.net Be sure to indicate which position(s) you're interested in.

 

 

 

 

Privacy Policy

We understand that your personal privacy is one of your biggest concerns and we are committed to protecting it. At La Bella Italiano, it is our policy not to distribute, sell, rent or in any way make available, your name, email address or any other personal information to any third party.
